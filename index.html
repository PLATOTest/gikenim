  • Support team coordination and participate in project ceremonies
  • Contribute to team documentation and knowledge sharing

Feedback

  • Actively seek feedback from peers and leads to enhance quality of work
  • Demonstrate openness to learning and constructive input

Each competency shows the specific behaviors expected at this level

Results/Output

  • Delivers assigned tasks reliably and on time
  • Shares status updates proactively
  • Asks clarifying questions when unclear
  • Identifies and escalates issues appropriately
  • Suggests practical solutions when problems arise

Communication

  • Keeps stakeholders informed with clear updates
  • Maintains professionalism in all interactions
  • Adapts communication style to audience needs
  • Documents key decisions and shares broadly

Teamwork

  • Supports team through coordination and documentation
  • Participates actively in team activities
  • Contributes to positive team culture
  • Contributes positively to team dynamics
  • Adapts to changing team needs and priorities

Professional Development

  • Actively engages in internal learning and development opportunities
  • Builds foundational project management, analysis, or delivery skills
  • Develops familiarity with core tools and methodologies used at PLATO
  • Demonstrates growth mindset and curiosity about new techniques
  • Seeks feedback to enhance professional growth

Leadership/Initiative

  • Demonstrates respectful communication
  • Shows accountability and willingness to learn
  • Takes initiative to improve processes
  • Identifies opportunities for improvement

Your Path to Consultant

What Changes at Consultant Level:

  • You'll own delivery outcomes for your assigned scope (not just tasks)

  • You'll manage delivery risks without constant oversight
